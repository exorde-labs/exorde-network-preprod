The Internet needs superheroes.

Exorde is the place where people fight against online misinformation.

Our enemy ? Fake news propagation.

In recent years, a great deal of attention has been devoted to the rise in fake news: everything from satire and misleading content to articles that are completely fabricated. When any type of content (image, news, video, audio, …) makes it on the web, it can spread anywhere in a matter of seconds, without any original context, for any purpose. Any authentic content (or not) can be reused to create viral fake information to manipulate the public opinion on a given subject in the short term or hurt the reputation of any entity (organizations, personas, etc).

Understand Exorde in a minute.

It's difficult, when surfing the internet, to know if what we read is reliable. Exorde aims to help you navigate serenely.

The Exorde platform will gather a whole community that will browse the web and its information. Accessible for free by everyone through a website, you will just have to create an account to access it. You will then have different tasks to perform to find, classify and link information on the web and allow the Exorde system to detect fake news.

For each contribution and when there is a consensus between the different collaborators, you will earn EXD, the cryptocurrency of Exorde. You will then be able to exchange it for euros, dollars, ethereum, bitcoin, etc.

The information entered on the platform will then be found on the Exorde browser extension. Accessible without registration, this extension will allow you to display a reliability indicator on the page you visit and will indicate other sites that contain similar information.

Brace yourselves, Exorde is coming.
